SPLASH - WHITE WATER RAFTING

With over 15,000 happy rafters and counting, we pride ourselves on creating unforgettable experiences for adventurers from all walks of life.

History

Splash White Water Rafting began as a dream in 2012 when three lifelong friends decided to turn their passion for adventure into a full-fledged business. Armed with a single raft, an old pickup truck, and boundless enthusiasm, they started guiding small groups down the Crystal River.
